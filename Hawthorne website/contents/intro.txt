Our story started like that of many others, in the city of Willow creek. Our great-grand parents whose name we have lost were very poor. Our great-grandmother at the time, was an amazing artist who owned a gallery, and her man, a scientist. He would experiment in his lab and take care of the rocketships there. One day he got abducted. He returned different. He bore a new alien baby to this world. Skylar Grey.
Skylar Grey is where our story starts. Her sister Adelle had to take her, move away, and never look back. She disguised Skylar. Adelle only ever had one man, but didn't love him. They did have a child though - Sebastian. Sebastian and Skylar lived as brother and sister and this is where our firsthand stories can begin...
